How It Works

Four Ways to
Engage with GCTI

Access Ranked Talent

Browse pre-assessed candidates ranked across our transparent performance tiers (Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze). Match talent to your organisational needs and risk appetite.

Observe Before Hiring

Offer short-term projects, placements, or supervised exposure to GCTI participants. See how they perform in your actual environment before making a hiring commitment.

Evidence-Based Selection

Make informed decisions backed by continuous performance data across technical execution, reliability, communication, and security mindset — not just interviews or CV screening.

Reduce Recruitment Risk

Transparent performance differentiation means you know exactly what you're getting. No guesswork — just clear, consistent, independently assessed talent profiles.

The Value Proposition

Why GCTI Makes
Hiring Smarter

Continuously Assessed

Unlike one-off certifications, GCTI participants are evaluated across their entire 12-month journey. You receive a holistic performance profile, not a single snapshot.

Transparent Performance Data

Each participant has a verified performance tier based on observable, consistent criteria. No inflated credentials — just honest, reliable assessments you can trust.

Industry-Aligned Skills

GCTI curriculum is designed with employer needs in mind. Participants learn what organisations actually need — not what looks good on a brochure.
