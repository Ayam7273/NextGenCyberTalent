The Problem

Breaking Into Cybersecurity
Shouldn't Be This Hard

Theory Over Practice

Many learners watch tutorials and earn certificates, but lack real hands-on experience that employers actually demand when hiring.

Generic Training

One-size-fits-all courses ignore unique backgrounds, pace, and goals. Personalised mentorship is needed — not just video lectures.

Job Market Disconnect

Traditional programmes teach outdated skills. Graduates leave without practical experience or a portfolio to land their first role.

The Solution

A 12-Month Journey

Performance Framework

Transparent Talent Ranking

A tiered system so employers can engage talent at the right readiness level

Platinum

Consistently high performers. Strong technical execution, dependable delivery, and clear professional judgement. Ready for complex responsibilities.

Elite

Gold

Highly capable and reliable. Performs strongly across most domains with minimal supervision needed.

Advanced

Silver

Solid foundational capability. Demonstrates growing confidence and benefits from guided exposure to real environments.

Developing

Bronze

Early-stage learners. Actively developing core skills within a structured, supervised environment.
